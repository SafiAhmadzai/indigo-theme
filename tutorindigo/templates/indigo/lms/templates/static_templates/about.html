About iMMAP Inc.

iMMAP Inc. is an international nonprofit organization that has been delivering information management services to humanitarian and development organizations in Afghanistan since 2007.

Our mission is to empower partners to make informed decisions that result in high-quality, targeted assistance for the world’s most vulnerable populations.

We offer a comprehensive range of services, including capacity strengthening, tailored to enhance the abilities of technical and non-technical stakeholders in information management. Our focus areas in capacity strengthening encompass critical sectors such as food security, health, disaster risk reduction, and more.
